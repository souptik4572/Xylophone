Playing the Instrument

There are mainly 8 keys in this musical instrument. And in order to make playing the instrument a little bit easy, we have added individual keys of the keyboard which will play the different notes.

C-major is represented by capital-A
D-major is represented by capital-S
E-major is represented by capital-D
F-major is represented by capital-F
G-major is represented by capital-H
A-major is represented by capital-J
B-major is represented by capital-K
C-minor is represented by capital-L

Or you can use just click the appropriate tile to play the notes.
